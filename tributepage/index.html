Moshoeshoe I

Ntate oa Basotho, moshoeshoeilana

Here is a time line of King Moshoeshoe's life

  • 1786 - Born in the village of Menkoaneng.
  • 1810 - Moshoeshoe married Mabela the daughter of the Bafokeng.
  • 1833 - He has 30 wives and it allowed him forge alliances with other chefs and increased his wealth.
  • 1822 - Moshoeshoe led the Basotho nation from Butha Buthe to Thaba Bosiu.
  • 1843 - Moshoeshoe signed a treaty with the governor of the British Cape Colony Sir George Napier.
  • 1848 - He was pressured into signing an agreement whereby he recognised British paramount authority over lands north of the Orange River.
  • 1866 - He signed the Treaty of Thaba Bosiu, whereby Moshoeshoe ceded most of his kingdom's arable land to the Boers
